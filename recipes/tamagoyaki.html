Tamagoyaki

Description

It's easy to make authentic Japanese food at home with this classic Japanese omelette recipe. Tamagoyaki is slightly sweet and seasoned with soy sauce, mirin, and dashi stock.

It's a favorite in bento boxes and also makes an easy Japanese appetizer. Serve with shredded daikon and soy sauce for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons prepared dashi stock
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
  • ½ teaspoon soy sauce
  • ½ tablespoon vegetable oil
