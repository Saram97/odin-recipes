Japanese Soup with Tofu and Mushrooms

Description

Miso is a fermented soy bean paste that adds a rich flavor.
This is a quick, healthy soup that is very popular in Japanese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups prepared dashi stock
  • ¼ cup sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • ⅛ cup cubed soft tofu
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring stock to a boil; reduce heat to simmer, add mushrooms, and cook for 3 minutes.
    In a small bowl, mix miso paste and soy sauce together; stir into stock along with tofu.
    Simmer 5 minutes, and serve topped with with green onion.
